# Privacy Policy — Only The Recipe

**Last updated: 2026-05-12**

Only The Recipe is built around a simple promise: your recipes stay on your device. This policy describes exactly what the app does and does not collect.

## What the app stores locally

When you save a recipe, the app stores the following on your device only:

- The recipe content extracted from the page (title, ingredients, instructions, image URL, source URL, timing, equipment, author notes).
- Any personal notes you add to a saved recipe.
- Your grocery list items.
- Your preferences (e.g., metric / imperial toggle).

These are kept in your device's private app storage and your device's Android Backup service (if you have backup enabled on your phone). They are never transmitted to us or to any third party.

## What the app does not collect

- We do not have user accounts. You do not sign in.
- We do not collect personal information — no name, email, phone, or contacts.
- We do not collect analytics, telemetry, crash data, or usage statistics.
- We do not track your location.
- We do not read or send your saved recipes anywhere off your device.

## Network requests the app makes

The app contacts the internet only in these specific cases:

1. **Loading a recipe page you ask it to load.** When you paste a URL or share one into the app, your device fetches that page from the website — exactly like opening it in a browser. The website you're loading may set cookies or run analytics; that is between you and that website, and is not under our control. We do not see those requests.

2. **Image loading inside the saved-recipe view.** The hero image is loaded from the original recipe site each time you open the saved view.

3. **(Sideload version only) Update check.** If you installed the app via the GitHub release APK (not via Google Play), it checks GitHub's public API once per launch to see if a newer version exists. No personal data is sent — just a standard HTTP request to `api.github.com`. The Google Play version of the app does not perform this check; Play handles updates.

## Permissions the app requests

- **Internet** — to load recipe pages on your request.

The app does not request access to contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or any other sensitive permission.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If the app starts collecting additional information (for example, if banner ads are added in a future version), this policy will be updated before that change ships, with a new "Last updated" date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
